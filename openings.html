Zhaolab postdoc position

The Zhao lab is seeking highly motivated undergraduate students, graduate students and postdocs to join the lab to study protein structure, ligands, functions and mechanisms through various computational methods. Our lab has close collaborations with multidisciplinary labs at iHuman Institute and School of Life Science and Technology, as well as labs outside of ShanghaiTech University.

Research Directions:
1) Membrane protein ligand/drug discovery and mechanism study.
2) Enzyme substrate/product prediction and mechanism study; metabolic pathway prediction.

Please contact zhaosw(at)shanghaitech.edu.cn if you are interested.
